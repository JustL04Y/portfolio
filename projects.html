Blood Donation Managment System

This project focuses on creating a system to manage blood donations efficiently. It will help hospitals and blood banks organize donors, schedule appointments, and track inventory. The system will streamline operations, making them faster and safer, allowing blood banks to manage resources better and ultimately save more lives.
